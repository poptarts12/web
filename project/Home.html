​about our technology​

​We will use deep learning to analyze the video footage captured by a security camera in order to identify and classify unusual objects or activities, such as intruders or suspicious behavior. We will do this using techniques such as object detection, tracking objects, identifying the intruder based on his clothing, identifying the intruder based on his behavior and identifying objects that will hold, animal detection, etc., we will trust the deep learning model to identify objects, specific patterns in the video data, etc. After training the model, we will use it to automatically identify and track objects or activities in real time, allowing for more efficient and effective tracking. In addition to this, with the help of an algorithm we are developing, the AI will weight percentages according to the categories we presented here, thus achieving maximum accuracy and efficiency.
