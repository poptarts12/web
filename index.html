Q&A

What kind of cameras is Wolly compatible with?

Wolly can be connected to almost any device that delivers the RTSP video signal.
IP cameras with Onvif or IP cameras that are not Onvif compatible or even Analog cameras that are connected to a DVR which is capable to transmit RTSP signal

How does the installation process work?

The installation process is carried out when the camera owner gives us details about his camera and when he gives access we will make the connection ourselves



What equipment is needed to set up the Wolly platform on-site?

No equipment except for a security camera with an internet connection
